Repair FAQs

No, your data is fully preserved. We follow a strict security and technical repair protocol that does not touch your device's motherboard data storage. However, we always recommend making a backup before any hardware service as standard tech practice.
We use premium OEM-grade screen replacements and parts that match original factory specifications in color depth, touch sensitivity, refresh rates, and glass durability. We also offer original refurbished screens for select models.
Our 90-day warranty covers any parts replaced and our labor costs. If the replaced screen, battery, or charging port fails to perform correctly, we replace it free of charge. The warranty does not cover subsequent physical cracks, drops, or liquid damage after repair completion.
When you bring your phone, we will run comprehensive hardware tests at no charge to find the exact fault. We explain the issue and quote a repair price. If you choose not to proceed with the repair, there is absolutely zero diagnostic fee to pay.
